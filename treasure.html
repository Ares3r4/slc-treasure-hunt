Objective

The purpose of this event is to organize a fun and engaging treasure hunt activity for members of the Student Learning Community. It encourages problem-solving and exploration while bringing a festive and exciting challenge across campus.

Event Overview

The Treasure Hunt features five hidden locations around the campus. Each location can be found by scanning QR codes that lead to digital puzzles. When a puzzle is solved, the next location will be visible.

How It Works

  • Start at the designated starting point and scan the first QR code.
  • Each QR code opens a webpage with a puzzle of a familiar campus location.
  • Continue through all five stages to complete the hunt.
  • The first participant to finish all puzzles and reach the final spot wins!

  • To claim the prize, the winner must show proof of all five puzzles solved.
